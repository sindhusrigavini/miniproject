ABOUT Mehrangarh

Mehrangarh Fort is a magnificent fortress in Jodhpur, Rajasthan, India. Built in 1459 by Rao Jodha, the founder of Jodhpur, it stands 400 feet above the city on a rocky hill. The fort is known for its massive walls, intricate carvings, and expansive courtyards. Inside, you'll find several palaces, including the Moti Mahal (Pearl Palace) and Phool Mahal (Flower Palace), which showcase exquisite architecture. The fort also houses a museum displaying weapons, paintings, and royal artifacts
