ABOUT JAMMU AND KASHMIR

Jammu and Kashmir is a breathtaking region in northern India, offering a blend of natural beauty, rich culture, and spiritual significance. Srinagar, the summer capital, is known for its iconic Dal Lake, where houseboats and shikaras provide a serene experience, and the Mughal gardens reflect the grandeur of historic architecture. Gulmarg, a winter wonderland, is famous for skiing and adventure sports, attracting thrill-seekers from across the globe. Pahalgam, with its lush green meadows and scenic landscapes, is a popular destination for nature lovers and trekkers. Sonmarg, or the "Meadow of Gold," is surrounded by majestic glaciers and serves as the gateway to Ladakh. Jammu is home to the revered Vaishno Devi shrine, a pilgrimage site visited by millions. Lesser-known gems like Yusmarg, Gurez Valley, and Verinag offer tranquility and untouched beauty, making them perfect for offbeat travel. Whether exploring historic sites, trekking through the Himalayas, or simply soaking in the mesmerizing views, Jammu and Kashmir promises an unforgettable experience.
